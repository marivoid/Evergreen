About us

Evergreen is a young company looking to help those who are unsure which plants suit them best for their homes and lifestyle. We encourage our customers to decorate their homes with plants to liven up their space and bring life in their everyday environment. Some might just be in need for the natural color while others might wish to have a small daily task of taking care of their plant friend. Below you will find the two currently available options of plants, each suitable for a different lifestyle and personality.

Our plants

Prayer Plant

The Prayer Plant is great for people who are worried that their plant might not survive without sunlight. This lovely plant does not need sunlight to thrive! With that being said, it can be anywhere in your home. The reason why it's called the Prayer Plant is because, once nightfall, the plant's leaves will fold as if they are praying.

Pothos Plant

If you're a beginner plant owner this plant is perfect for you! Pothos, also known as Devil's Ivy, are very easy to grow and take care of. This beautiful and durable plant thrives best in indirect light and in draft free environments. It can grow perfectly fine in low light and needs to have moist soil.
